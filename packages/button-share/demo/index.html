ButtonShare.js Demo

Your browser may not support navigator.share().

Check the supported environment with Can I use....

no attribute

<button type="button" class="js-button-share">Share</button>

* The title is obtained from document.title and the url is obtained from document.URL. The text will be an empty string.

data-share-title, data-share-text, data-share-url attribute

<button type="button" class="js-button-share" data-share-text="Message text" data-share-title="Page title" data-share-url="/path/to">Share</button>
