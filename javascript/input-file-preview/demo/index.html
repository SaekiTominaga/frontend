InputFilePreview.js Demo

data-preview, data-error-message attribute

<input type="file" class="js-file-preview"
  data-preview="preview1"
  data-error-message='&lt;strong class="error"&gt;&lt;span class="filename"&gt;${name}&lt;/span&gt; (${size} byte) cannot be previewed.&lt;/strong&gt;'
/>
<p id="preview1"></p>

* Files larger than 10 MiB or files in formats other than image / audio / video will not be previewed and an error message will be displayed.

multiple, data-preview, data-error-message, data-max-size attribute

<input type="file" multiple="" class="js-file-preview"
  data-preview="preview2"
  data-error-message='&lt;strong class="error"&gt;&lt;span class="filename"&gt;${name}&lt;/span&gt; (${size} byte) cannot be previewed.&lt;/strong&gt;'
  data-max-size="1048576"
/>
<ul id="preview2"></ul>

    * Files larger than 1 MiB (data-max-size="1048576") or files in formats other than image / audio / video will not be previewed and an error message will be displayed.
